NP Membership Benefits

Non-profit organizations are offered complimentary membership to the Chamber, providing access to a wide range of exclusive resources designed to enhance their operations and outreach. These include free access to workshops, networking events, and special grants that can assist with operational and fundraising efforts. This membership ensures that non-profits can grow and engage with other businesses and organizations for greater community impact without worrying about membership fees.

×

Bronze Membership Benefits

With a Bronze membership, you gain access to essential benefits that help your business or organization grow. Members at this level enjoy discounts on Chamber-hosted events, including conferences, workshops, and networking opportunities. Additionally, Bronze members receive training resources aimed at helping them improve business operations, from marketing tips to financial management strategies. It's an affordable way to get started and begin building valuable connections within the business community.

×

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver members enjoy an enhanced set of benefits that provide more exposure and specialized resources. In addition to all the Bronze membership perks, Silver members have access to exclusive events, including VIP networking sessions and industry-specific seminars. You'll also receive more targeted marketing support, such as promotional opportunities through the Chamber‚Äôs platforms, special features in the Chamber's newsletter, and social media shoutouts. This membership tier is ideal for businesses looking to increase their visibility and access advanced training and support.

Gold Membership Benefits

The Gold Membership offers the highest level of benefits available, designed for businesses and organizations looking for maximum exposure and exclusive support. Gold members receive all the perks from the Silver and Bronze memberships, plus added benefits like prominent spotlight positions on the Chamber's website, a higher priority for event invitations, and additional discounts on event sponsorships. Furthermore, Gold members can enjoy one-on-one consulting sessions with industry experts, access to a dedicated account manager, and first-in-line access to special promotions and opportunities. This tier is perfect for businesses that want to stand out and maximize their engagement with the Chamber and the broader business community.
